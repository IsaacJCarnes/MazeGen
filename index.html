The Maze Runner

×

After selecting an animal and clicking the start button, you will start at the top left corner of a maze and have to make your way down to the bottom right corner of the maze. After you complete a maze, your character will be loaded into a new maze. This procress will repeated until you have completed all mazes. See how fast you can finish!

This game uses WASD key controls meaning:

  • Press the W key to move up
  • Press the A key to move left
  • Press the S key to move down
  • Press the D key to move right

If your screen is small enough, directional buttons will be displayed to move your character.
